Quick setup

  1. Replace REPLACE_WITH_YOUR_EMAIL in the form's action attribute with your real email (for example: https://formsubmit.co/you@domain.com).
  2. Optionally verify the sender email following Formsubmit.co's instructions (they may send a confirmation email first).

If you don't want to expose your email in HTML, the form will fallback to opening your email client (mailto) if the action isn't configured — see note below after submission.
