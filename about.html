About Me

Hello! I am Max Ricci, a 19-year-old student based in Lugano, Switzerland. My passion lies in creating innovative designs that blend functionality with aesthetics. With a background in both design and art, I strive to push the boundaries of traditional design thinking.

My work focuses on exploring the relationship between time, space, and human perception. I believe that good design should not only serve a purpose but also evoke emotions and inspire creativity.

Feel free to explore my portfolio and reach out if you'd like to collaborate or learn more about my work!
